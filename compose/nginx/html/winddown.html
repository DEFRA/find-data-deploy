Find data

The Find Data service, which was an Alpha prototype set up by the Defra Data Strategy team, is closing down with effect from Monday 10th June 2019.

The service is being closed down to allow for agreement on a shared strategic approach to finding data with appropriate business and product ownership and suitable governance.

The service is being packaged up to facilitate restart at a later date. We are establishing a community of interest for later discussions on the product. There will be summary artefacts available after closedown including a presentation and updated demo video to support next steps.

Please note that after closedown all communications about the project should be routed via Andrew Newman (andrew.newman@defra.gov.uk)

Thank you for your interest in and support of the project.

The Find Data Team
